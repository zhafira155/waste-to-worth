Waste is the remaining material or waste material from human activities that is no longer wanted or used after a process. Waste can be in solid, liquid, or gas form, and can be distinguished based on its source (domestic, industrial, agricultural) and its compounds (organic, inorganic, Yusuf or hazardous and toxic materials/B3). Waste can have a negative impact on the environment and human health if not managed properly.
