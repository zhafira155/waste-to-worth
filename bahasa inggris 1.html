Turning Jerrycan Waste into Shelves


Around us, jerrican waste is often just thrown away after use. In fact, used jerricans can actually be repurposed into more useful and valuable products. One form of utilization is by processing used jerrican waste into multipurpose shelves.


Used jerricans can be transformed into various types of shelves with different shapes and functions. For example, the jerrican can be cut on the sides, then assembled and stacked to resemble a multipurpose shelf. Shelves made from jerricans can be used to store books, shoes, school supplies, or kitchen utensils. In addition to being strong and durable, jerrican shelves are also easy to clean because they are made of plastic material that is not easily damaged


In addition, using jerrycans as shelves can also beautify the appearance of a room when combined with creativity. Jerrycans can be painted and combined with other used materials to make them look more aesthetic. As a result, the shelves are not only functional but can also become part of home decor. This is what makes jerrycan shelves a suitable choice, as they provide both function and art.


Utilizing jerrycan waste as shelves also has the potential to open up business opportunities. With low capital and simple assembly techniques, jerrycan shelf products can be sold to people who need multipurpose shelves. Therefore, creativity in transforming jerrycan waste into multipurpose shelves can be developed as an innovation that benefits both the environment and the economy
